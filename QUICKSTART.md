## 🎨 Customization (Optional but Recommended)

### Add Your Photos (5 minutes)
1. Find 2-3 nice couple/together photos
2. Resize them to around 280x280px (square)
3. Save as JPG format
4. Place in `assets/images/` folder:
   - `image1.jpg` (left side of letter)
   - `image2.jpg` (right side of letter)
   - `cats.gif` (celebration section - optional cute cats)

### Add Background Music (3 minutes)
1. Find a romantic instrumental song (1-3 min)
2. Convert/save as MP3 format
3. Place in `assets/audio/` folder:
   - `background-music.mp3`
4. Music will auto-play with toggle button 🔊
